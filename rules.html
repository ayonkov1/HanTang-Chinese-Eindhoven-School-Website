Rules

1. Every Student Should Be On Time At School Every Saturday And, If Unable To Attend, Must Notify A Teacher.

2. Each Student Must Regularly Do Homework In Their Own Interest.

3. The Teachers Regularly Give Tests. There Are Exams In January And At The End Of The School Year. Reports Are Also Handed Out To The Students.

4. Mobile Phones And Gaming Consoles Are Prohibited In Class, Unless Told Otherwise By The School Representative In The Class.

5. Dolores Jane Umbridge (High Inquisitor) Has Replaced Albus Dumbledore As The Head Of Hogwarts School Of Witchcraft And Wizardry

6. Any Student Found In Possession Of Sweets From Unauthorized Suppliers Will Be Expelled.

7. Wands To Be Confiscated From Students Casting Recreational Spells. All Magical Objects To Be Held By High Inquisitor Until Further Notice

8. Boys & Girls Are Not Permitted To Be Within 6 Inches Of Each Other.

9. Any Items Deemed Not Of Educational Value Are Henceforth Banned.

10. Any Literature By Non-Wizards Or Half Breeds Is Banned Forthwith.
