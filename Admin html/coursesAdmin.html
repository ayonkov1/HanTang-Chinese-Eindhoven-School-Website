Youngsters and children Mandarin (儿童 少年 普通话 班)

Classes for toddlers level 1 and 2. Youth classes from levels 1 to 10 and sometimes higher. The lessons are structured systematically. The textbooks are written by experts in China. The students are supervised and examined by our teachers. The teaching material is included in the course price.

Chinese (Mandarin) courses for adults (9:15 - 11:45)

Chinese (Mandarin) courses for adults (9: 15-11: 45): We offer courses that are also suitable for adults (成人). The lessons for adults have a structure that assumes that the lessons are regularly attended and that homework and tests are also done. This is the only way to benefit from the lessons. No knowledge of Chinese is required for these courses. Someone who already knows some Chinese can enter at a higher level, in consultation with the school management.

Mandarin Course for Adults (成人 普通话 班)

A 4-year course. The teaching material is speaking and writing. The teaching material is a multi-volume textbook (新 实用 汉语, “New Practical Chinese Reader”) from a Chinese language institute in Beijing. All explanations in these books are in English. CD and workbook are included. Note: In the class of the second and third year there is also practice for the HSK exam.
