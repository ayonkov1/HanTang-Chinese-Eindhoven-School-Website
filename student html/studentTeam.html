Wu Yanwei (YW Ng, 吴炎威)

He has been involved in the Eindhoven Chinese School from the very beginning. Since 1984, Mr. Ng has been vice-chairman of the board of the Sino-Indian Companies sector of the ... Koninklijke Horeca Nederland. Mister Ng is known for his cooking skills. Since 1986, he has been responsible for the national Chinese cookery courses, and he is also a jury member within the sector. From 1993 he is a board member of the Oriental Cuisine Association, where he is a team leader of Chinese cooking and culinary development. From 1996 to 2000 he gave information meetings as a volunteer at the Wah Fook Wui Foundation. He is an advisor or chairman of various Chinese associations in the Eindhoven area. Because he is an important leader of the Chinese community in the Netherlands and has done much for the integration of Chinese into Dutch society, he received a Royal Honor in 2004. He was appointed a Member of the Order of Orange-Nassau.
